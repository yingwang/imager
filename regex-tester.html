Free Online Regex Tester

SnapTools Regex Tester lets you write and test regular expressions with real-time matching and visual highlighting. See matches highlighted in your test string, inspect capture groups, and experiment with flags — all in your browser.

Supports JavaScript regex syntax with global (g), case-insensitive (i), multiline (m), and dotAll (s) flags.

Frequently Asked Questions

What regex flavor is used?

This tool uses JavaScript's built-in RegExp engine. The syntax is compatible with most common regex patterns used in JavaScript, Python, and other languages.

What are capture groups?

Capture groups are portions of a regex pattern enclosed in parentheses. They allow you to extract specific parts of a match. This tool shows all capture groups for each match in the Match Details panel.
