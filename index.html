Why SnapTools?

SnapTools is a collection of free online image tools that run entirely in your browser. Unlike other services, we never upload your images to any server. All processing happens locally on your device using modern browser APIs, which means your photos stay private and secure.

Whether you need to compress images for your website, convert formats for compatibility, resize photos for social media, or add watermarks to protect your work — SnapTools has you covered. No registration, no watermarks, no limits.

Built with modern web technologies including Canvas API, WebAssembly, and advanced image processing algorithms. Works on desktop and mobile browsers.
