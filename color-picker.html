Frequently Asked Questions

What color formats are supported?

HEX (#RRGGBB), RGB (rgb(r, g, b)), and HSL (hsl(h, s%, l%)) formats. Click "Copy" next to any format to copy it to your clipboard.

What are the palette types?

Complementary shows the opposite color on the color wheel. Analogous shows neighboring colors. Triadic shows three evenly-spaced colors. Shades shows lighter and darker variations of your color.
